
CUBAN LEADER CASTRO RECEIVES YOUTH DELEGATION

SK106849 Pyongyang KCNA in English 0843 GMT 16 Feb 84

[Text] Pyongyang February 16 (KCNA) -- Comrade Fidel Castro Ruz, first
secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba and
president of the State Council of the Republic of Cuba, on February 10
received the delegation of the League of Socialist Working Youth of our
country headed by Kim Song-chol, vice-chairman of its Central Committee.
The head of the delegation conveyed cordial regards of the great leader
Comrade Kim II-song to the first secretary. The first secretary expressed
deep thanks for this and asked the head of the delegation to convey his
heartfelt greetings to the great leader. The parties and peoples of Cuba
and Korea are brothers struggling shoulder to shoulder against imperialism,
he noted, and stressed: The Cuban people always extend full support to the
struggle of the Korean people against U.S. imperialism. The conversation
proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
