
Husak, Strougal Join Talks

Prague CTK International Service in English 1712 GMT 24 Jun 72 L

[Text] Bratislava June 24 CETEKA--Dr Gustav Husak, general secretary of the
Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, and Federal Premier Lubomir Strougal
arrived in Poprad in the High Tatras this afternoon to join the visiting
Cuban party leader and Premier Fidel Castro in his trip to the northern
Slovak mountain range.
