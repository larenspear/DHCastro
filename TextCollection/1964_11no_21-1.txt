
Labor Union Support

Havana Domestic Service in Spanish 2300 GMT 21 November 1964--F

(Text) Aware of the great efforts which the Revolutionary Government is
trying out in the educational field, the workers of the National Food
Industry Labor Union announced their enthusiastic support for the proposal
made by Premier Fidel Castro that each worker and student contribute one
peso a month to partly pay for the national campaign for the conquest of
the sixth grade.
