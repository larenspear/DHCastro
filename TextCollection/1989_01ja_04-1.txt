
Castro Promises More Releases

FL0401114889 Bonaire Trans World Radio in English
1130 GMT 4 Jan 89

[Text] Foreign leaders continue to arrive in Havana, Cuba for festivities
marking the 30th anniversary of the Cuban Revolution.  Among those
attending is Nicaraguan President Daniel Ortega.  The week-long
celebrations end today with an exhibit on the results of 30 years of
socialism.

Cuban President Castro has promised the release of the remaining 225
prisoners that his government recognizes as political.
