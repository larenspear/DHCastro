
Statement on Television

Santiago Chile PRENSA LATINA in Spanish to PRENSA LATINA Havana 2030 GMT 10
Nov 71 C--FOR OFFICIAL USE ONLY

[Text] Santiago, Chile, 10 Nov--"I can say that I am very happy and deeply
moved to be in Chile," said Cuban Prime Minister Fidel Castro in a short
statement over the state television channel at the Pudahuel Airport today.

"We shall meet again and talk later on," said Fidel Castro to a woman
reporter.
