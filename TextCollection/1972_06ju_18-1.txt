
Tours Animal Research Center

East Berlin ADN International Service in German 1739 GMT 18 Jun 72 L

[Summary] On Sunday afternoon, thousands of people welcomes Castro and the
other members of the Cuban delegation at the research center for animal
production at Dummersdorf near Rostock. After talking with experts, Castro
said that he thought that Cuba and the GDR should exchange experience in
this field. It might be interesting for researchers from the GDR to carry
out experiments in Cuba under different climatic conditions. He stressed:
"It is our endeavor and intention to develop relations with the GDR in all
spheres, and we are certain that these developments will have brilliant
prospects."
