
FRENCH PRESIDENT ENVOY ARRIVES ON OFFICIAL VISIT

FL011125Y Havana Domestic Service in Spanish 1100 GMT 1 Dec 77 FL

[Text] Micheal Poniatowski, special representative of the French president
has arrived here on a special flight for an official visit.  Foreign
Minister Isidora Malmiercaf and French Ambassador to Cuba Dimitri de
Favitski, among others, received Poniatowski at Jose Marti International
Airport.
