
ADAM RAPACKI, FIDEL CASTRO CONFER

Havana in Spanish to the Americas 1400 GMT 14 June 1962--E

(Text) Adam Rapacki, Foreign Minister of the Polish People's Republic, has
been received by Premier of Fidel Castro of the revolutionary government.
During a cordial and extensive talk they exchanged experiences on problems
of agriculture and other aspects of building socialism in the two
countries. The highest leader of the Cuban revolution, Maj. Fidel Castro,
expressed a desire to tighten and strengthen relations between Poland and
Cuba.

Adam Rapacki thanked him for this statement and said that the Polish
leaders have the same interest in this. On taking leave, the Polish foreign
minister repeated an invitation to the Cuban Premier to visit his country.
