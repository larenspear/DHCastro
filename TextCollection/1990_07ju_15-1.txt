
FULL TEXT OF ARTICLE:
1.  [Text] In a conversation with foreign correspondents at the French Embassy,
which was celebrating its national day, Cuban President Fidel Castro guaranteed
the security of diplomatic missions in the country.

2.  Fidel Castro said that people cannot be allowed to forcibly enter
embassies, and he described the refugees as antisocial elements without any
patriotic motivation.

3.  Castro also said that Cuba will continue to receive Soviet aid because
Mikhail Gorbachev does not accept U.S.  demands to end it.
