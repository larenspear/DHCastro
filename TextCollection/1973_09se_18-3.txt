
Meeting With Husak, Departure

Prague Domestic Service in Czech/Slovak 0100 GMT 18 Sep 73 L

[Excerpt] Gustav Husak, general secretary of the CPCZ Central Committee,
gave a gala dinner in honor of Comrade Castro and his entourage at Ruzyne
Airport, which took place in a exceptionally friendly and cordial
atmosphere.

During their meeting, Comrades Castro and Husak voiced, on behalf of their
respective parties, full solidarity with the Chilean people who have become
the victims of a coup against the legal government of Popular Unity.

A few hours later Comrade Castro left Prague and continued his journey
home.
