FBIS-LAT-94-156 Daily Report 12 Aug 1994 CARIBBEAN Cuba
Roundup of Politburo Members' Activities 
FL1008115194 FL1008115194 BFN 

[Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on the activities of members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba monitored from 2 to 9 August. 

Roberto Robaina met members of the sixth contingent of the Canadian Solidarity With Cuba Brigade on 2 August. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 2 Aug 94) 

Yadira Garcia Vera, first secretary of the Communist Party in Matanzas, will chair a ceremony where citrus farms workers from Union de Reyes and Jaguey Grande Municipalities will announce production pledges for the citrus campaign which starts today. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 9 Aug 94) 

Julian Rizo Alvarez, first secretary of the Communist Party in Camaguey, visited the Camaguey Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology Center to evaluate the current situation, and the prospects of research projects on food production. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 9 Aug 94) 

Candido Palmero Hernandez, first secretary of the Communist Party in Havana, stated the need to speed up soil preparation for the winter planting campaign. Palmero visited the vegetable and tuber farms in Artemisa, Alquizar, Guira de Melena, and San Antonio de los Banos Municipalities. Palmero "criticized the bad work performed in plantain farms in Alquizar, which used the microjet system, and which are under the charge of the Youth Labor Army." (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 9 Aug 94) 

Alfredo Jordan chaired in Villa Clara Province a meeting of the province's Communist Party of Cuba. Jordan stressed the need to increase food production efforts. Jordan wrapped up his visit with a trip to the province's agricultural production areas. (Havana Radio Reloj Network in Spanish 1933 GMT 9 Aug 94) 

Alfredo Hondal awarded the workers of the Martires Del Moncada Poultry Farm, in Ciego de Avila. Hondal praised the workers for their efficiency and production efforts. (Havana Radio Reloj Network in Spanish 1946 GMT 9 Aug 94)
