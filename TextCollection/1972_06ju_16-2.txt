
Visits Theater, Townhall

East Berlin ADN International Service in German 1351 GMT 16 Jun 72 L

[Summary] Fidel Castro, Werner Lamberz and Werner Krolikowski had a cordial
conversation with building workers at the new movie theater. At the
townhall, Werner Krolikowski and Mayor Gerhard Schill explained the history
and present state of Dresden to Fidel Castro. He learned that 50,000 new
dwellings have been built in Dresden. In the Palace of Culture, its
manager, Heinz Rottgard, reported on the work of this home of professional
and lay artists. Fidel Castro and his escort later returned to the Neva
Hotel.
