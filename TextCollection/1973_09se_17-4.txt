
CASTRO: CHILEAN COUP A TEMPORARY SETBACK

Delhi General Overseas Service in English 1000 GMT 17 Sep 73 D

[Text] Cuban Prime Minister Fidel Castro has said that the imperialists
have overthrown the democratic Government of Chile. He said he was sure
that this will be a temporary setback; but the Chilean people and workers
will fight back [against] the fascist regime. Dr. Castro was addressing a
gathering which had collected at the Calcutta airport this morning when his
plane arrived there.
