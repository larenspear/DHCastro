
Tours Construction Site

FL0711154888 Havana Television Tele-Rebelde Network in Spanish 1200 GMT 7
Nov 88

[Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro Ruz, first secretary of the PCC
Central Committee and president of the Councils of State and Ministers,
visited the Expo-Cuba site where 4,000 construction workers were carrying
out their volunteer day this Red Sunday.

Fidel chatted with the workers, the majority of whom were working on the
central pavilion.  He also expressed interest in the progress of various
other areas and access routes to the installation which is scheduled to be
completed on 31 December in salute to the 30th anniversary of the triumph
of the revolution.

The following members of the PCC Central Committee were also present at the
site: Jorge Lezcano, first secretary of the provincial committee of the
part in Havana City; Pedro Chavez, president of the Provincial Assembly of
the People's Government; and other leaders of the Central Organization of
Cuban Trade Unions in the capital.
